Translating Analytics into Viral Engagement

HYROX racing is highly analytical, centered on pacing, splits, and station split strategies. HighRocks was built to solve pacing guesswork, and our SMM strategy focused on communicating this data visually. We turned complex race analytics into digestible pacing guides and clean, shareable infographics on Instagram.

Using reels, we highlighted training splits, athlete breakdowns, and tool tutorials that fit native mobile formats. On Threads, we built interactive Q&As where athletes could get custom pacing splits, transforming the channel into a conversational utility that drove high click-through rates back to the tool.

"Data visualization and interactive tools drive organic saves." — We created high-value pacing cards that athletes saved and shared, leveraging interactive thread discussions to build HighRocks' reputation as a pacing expert.
