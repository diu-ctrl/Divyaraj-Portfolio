Esports Marketing & Fan Dynamics

Esports marketing is driven by raw hype, competitive stakes, and player-fan connections. The core objective with Gujarat Tigers Esports was to transform individual player fandoms into a collective, passionate team identity, rallying fans around tournament matchdays.

To achieve this, we developed a fast-paced short-form video framework. By clipping tournament live streams within minutes of a clutch squad-wipe or headshot, and applying custom audio synchronizations, dynamic zooms, and gaming visual effects, we pushed high-intent gaming reels directly to the algorithms.

"Capitalizing on crucial matchday moments to drive viral reach." — Leveraging actual tournament play and syncing SMM releases with live leaderboard hype drove record engagement, securing consistent 100K+ and up to 820K+ views on custom gaming edits.
