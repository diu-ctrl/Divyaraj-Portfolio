The Strategy & Corporate Positioning

B2B marketing for business intelligence engines requires a highly authoritative and informative communication model. Startups, banks, and corporate analysts do not seek hype; they seek trust, reliable data, and actionable intelligence.

Our approach with Finanvo focused on translating complex corporate research capabilities (GST lookup, legal registry tracking, director verification, EPF analytics) into highly readable, educative content sets. By utilizing LinkedIn carousels and industry thought-leadership articles, we built Prabhaat Somani and the core Finanvo brand into key figures on corporate compliance and private market statistics.

Simultaneously, we established an authority presence on external publication platforms. Crafting high-intent answers on Quora, writing analysis-backed breakdowns on Medium, and curating trends on Tumblr drove direct backlink referrals and organic B2B product registrations.

"Simplifying corporate search into digestible B2B insight." — Synchronizing executive updates on LinkedIn/X with targeted articles on Medium and Quora transformed corporate compliance topics into organic traffic drivers, scaling monthly impressions past 350K.
