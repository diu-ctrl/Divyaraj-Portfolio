The Problem Boundary

Most interval fitness timers on the market suffer from severe design flaws: accidental session termination due to single-tap exits, timer drift when multitasking, and absolute session data loss on phone calls or OS interruptions.

Shift was engineered as an interruption-safe, audio-primary state engine to solve these structural issues, offering 0% accidental workout cancellation guards, offline persistence, and asynchronous, non-intrusive monetization.

The SMM Launch Strategy

Because Shift is a new brand, our organic strategy focused heavily on educating home workout, HIIT, and Tabata enthusiasts about its key structural features. I structured campaigns around four core vision pillars:

"Workout timers shouldn't get in your way. Our social strategy highlighted Shift's Zero-Ad active session execution and 100% interruption-safe persistence — targeting zero setup friction for high-intensity exertion."

By positioning Shift as a clean, state-driven fitness tool rather than a generic ad-heavy timer, we quickly established high-intent community interest on Instagram and Threads.
