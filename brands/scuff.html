Apparel Branding & Lifestyle SMM

In the lifestyle and apparel space, social media is the primary storefront. SMM for Scuff focused on translating physical design and aesthetic vibes into a digital presence. We pivoted the content strategy towards visual-heavy, high-aspiration grid posts on Instagram, combined with active community-building threads.

By designing cohesive graphic frameworks, visual drop concepts, and high-impact lifestyle reels, we grew the audience organically. Leveraging Threads allowed the brand to drop the corporate facade and engage directly with street culture enthusiasts, establishing a genuine brand personality.

"Cohesive brand voice and high-aesthetic grid styling drive loyalty." — Unifying the product drop strategy with visual storytelling and direct thread engagement established Scuff as an active community brand, not just a label.

Creative Reels & SMM Concepts
Reel Concept 1

"I moved to Germany for a year. Coming back to India broke something in me."

The hook that starts the entire 30-day narrative. Personal, cinematic, and emotional. No product mention. No logo until the very end. This reel earns attention — it doesn't ask for it.

Reel Concept 2

"I counted how many times I opened Instagram yesterday. The number scared me."

Raw, single-shot talking head. No cuts. No b-roll. No music. Just you, a camera, and an uncomfortable truth. This is the reel that gets the most comments — because it asks people to be honest about something they've been avoiding.
