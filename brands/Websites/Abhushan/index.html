Goldsmiths, Not Marketers

Abhushan started in a small workshop in the old city of Ahmedabad, not in a boardroom. Our founder, Divyaraj, learned goldsmithing from traditional master craftsmen in Gujarat, and opened the first studio with one bench, one torch, and a belief that fine jewelry should be made where it's sold.

We still hand-finish every piece in that same neighborhood. The craftsmen who set your emeralds and weld your Forever chains are the same people who answer your questions when you call. We don't outsource. We don't dropship. We make things.

Our gold is 100% recycled — not because it's trendy, but because wasting gold feels wrong when you understand how much care goes into working it. Our gemstones are traceable to their source. Our packaging is hand-wrapped in the studio, not in a factory.

This is not fast fashion. This is not AI-generated. This is a group of people in Ahmedabad who believe that the things you wear every day should be made with the same care you'd give to the people you love.

— Divyaraj, Founder & Head Goldsmith
