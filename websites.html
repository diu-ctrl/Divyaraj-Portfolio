✦

✦

Collaborative Development & Vibe

✦

DESIGN + FRONTEND DEVELOPMENT

Detailing Cars (DC)

A premium, dark-mode digital showroom and scheduler for Ahmedabad's #1 mobile detailing service. Designed with precision-garage aesthetics to deliver a seamless online booking experience.

✦

Branding & Visual Direction

We established a high-contrast dark aesthetic that mirrors a surgical-grade detailing studio. Using pure black canvases to highlight high-gloss vehicle photography, the design is punctuated by vibrant gold accents for premium quality and performance blue for interactive feedback. Autonomic styling is reinforced by geometric Montserrat headings, monospaced JetBrains Mono typography, and precision-cut borders.

✦

Collaborative Development & Vibe

The core experience was built around an interactive paint-restoration before/after slider and an infinite moving gallery that showcases showroom-grade transformations. The scheduling engine features a dynamic three-tier selector adjusting for different vehicle classes, integrated with a hidden administrative control deck that allows secure, password-protected booking management behind discrete user-interaction triggers.

✦

Mobile Refinement & Launch

The layout transitions into a fluid, touch-optimized interface for mobile users, complete with sticky call-to-actions, tap-friendly modal overlays, and customized layout sizes. All vehicle assets were compressed to ensure fast load speeds, culminating in a successful web launch on Vercel.

Interactive Paint-Restoration Before/After Slider
Dynamic Three-Tier Vehicle Class Selector
Administrative control deck for reservation management
Touch-Optimized Mobile View with Sticky CTAs
